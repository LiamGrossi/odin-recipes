How to make pizza

This breakfast pita pocket is a good substitute for a plain old omelet.

You can use a variety of ingredients; however, I like the all-meat version. Serve with salsa and ketchup.

Ingredients

  • 2 links pork sausage
  • 2 slices Canadian-style bacon
  • 2 slices bacon
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 large pita bread, cut in half

Preparation

  1. Cook pork sausage, Canadian-style bacon, and bacon in a skillet over medium heat until evenly browned
  2. Crumble bacon; chop sausage and Canadian-style bacon.
  3. Crack eggs into the skillet, and cook over medium heat
  4. Meanwhile, microwave pita bread on high until warm, about 30 seconds.
